About Our Product

DripFix gives your everyday shoes, suede leather shoes, sneakers, and white shoes a lightning-fast wash. Twist the cap once to clean soles and shoelaces, and twist again for disinfection and odor removal. Your shoes feel fresh and brand new!

How It Works

1️⃣ Spray

Spray the DripFix solution directly on your shoes.

2️⃣ Brush

Use the brush to remove dirt from the surface and soles.

3️⃣ Fresh

Activate the disinfection mode to remove odor and bacteria.
